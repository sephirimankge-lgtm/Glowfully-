About GLOWFULLY

Glowfully Living is a wellness and self-care brand rooted in intentional living, gentle habits, and the belief that every person deserves to glow from the inside out. It was born from a deeply personal journey of rediscovery through our founder, Tshepo Maud Kgomo.

With over 20 years of experience in the corporate world - leading teams, strengthening operations, and driving transformation - Tshepo understands the pressure, burnout, and lifestyle imbalance many professionals silently carry. Her shift into wellness wasn't a career change
It was a calling. A personal awakening.

Several years ago, she was diagnosed as prediabetic and later hypertensive - two moments that forced her to pause and reconsider her lifestyle. Instead of giving in to fear, she chose healing and radical self-care. She embraced:

  • Long-distance running
  • A nutritious, whole-food way of eating
  • Intermittent fasting
  • Intentional rest
  • Mental and emotional wellbeing practices

Running became her discipline, her therapy, and her mirror. She went on to complete a number of long-distance races and openly shared the journey - the mindset, the endurance, the science, the emotional grit. Her reflections on Facebook and Instagram quietly built a community that resonated with her authenticity and consistency.

As her health improved and her glow returned, Tshepo realized something powerful: Wellness doesn't need to be complicated — it needs to be intentional. And so many people needed guidance from someone who understood that shift personally.

From that awakening, Glowfully Living was born - a sanctuary offering handcrafted self-care products, gentle coaching, and everyday wellness routines that support both women and men on their healing journeys.

Today, Tshepo creates handcrafted self-care products and serves as a compassionate lifestyle guide - helping women and men reset, rebalance and rediscover the joy of taking care of themselves. Through practical routines, gentle accountability, and a focus on everyday wellness, she supports people on a journey to glow from the inside out. Her work blends personal experience, empathy, mindfulness, and practical tools that make wellness feel accessible and achievable.

She believes every human should feel held, supported, and reminded of their inner light. Glowfully Living is the home she created to make that possible.
